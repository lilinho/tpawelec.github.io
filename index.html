Zipf's Law Check

Zipf's law states that given some corpus of natural language utterances, the frequency of any word is inversely proportional to its rank in the frequency table. (from Wikipedia). In this program I count word occurencies in given text file and present them on the graph next to estimated words frequency. For this moment program is not finished and has some bugs.
